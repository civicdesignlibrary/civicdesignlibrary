The Civic Design Library is a crowdsourced data set of civic design resources created by global practitioners working at the intersection of design and the public sector.

It aims to showcase the breadth and depth of civic design and offer data and visual formats for peer-to-peer learning and inspiration, research on the field, and sourcing content for events.

The data is available for any organization to use under Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International.

The library is curated by the Beeck Center for Social Impact + Innovation at Georgetown University and Rosenfeld Media.

It is inspired by resources such as the Social Change Directory, Civic Tech Companies and Organizations, and the Civic Tech Guide.
